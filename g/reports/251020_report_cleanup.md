## 🎯 Naming Convention Rules

### **Format:** `yymmdd_xxx.md`
- **yy:** Year (25 for 2025)
- **mm:** Month (10 for October)
- **dd:** Day (20 for 20th)
- **xxx:** Descriptive name (lowercase, underscores)

### **Examples:**
- `251020_ci_fixed_final.md`
- `251020_cli_secrets_success.md`
